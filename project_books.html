Books

Books provide a lasting way to share research, frameworks, and creative ideas that support teachers, students, and researchers. Our lab develops and contributes to books that make computer science, AI, and data science education more accessible for diverse learners and learning environments. These works highlight practical models, unplugged activities, and evidence-based strategies for teaching emerging technologies.

More publications and upcoming releases will be added soon.
