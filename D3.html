Welcome to D3 Playground!

A creative learning space designed especially for K–5 learners.

AI: Artificial Intelligence   C: Coding   E: Engineering   D: Data Science   R: Robotics   S: Security  

In today’s rapidly evolving world, technologies like Data Science and Artificial Intelligence are transforming the future. But what about young children? Often, they’re left out of the picture when it comes to learning these groundbreaking concepts. At D3 Playground Research Lab, we believe the journey into science and technology should start early, with tools and activities tailored just for them.

Our mission is simple yet impactful: to take complex ideas from computer science and technology and make them fun, engaging, and accessible for young learners. We design resources that spark curiosity, empower teachers, and involve parents in their child’s learning journey. Together, we’re building a future where every child has the opportunity to explore and thrive in the world of science and technology.

Let’s play a game together!

Can you help the fruit seller make an important decision? He's just starting his fruit shop and sells three kinds of fruits: apples, watermelons, and bananas.

Day One: It was a sunny day. He sold all the watermelons, some of the apples, and only a few bananas (the leftover bananas turned darker).

Day Two: It was cooler outside. He sold all the watermelons and bananas, along with some apples.

Now, he has enough money to buy only two types of fruit for the next day, which will be hot and sunny.
What two fruits should he buy to make the most sales?

Be a data detective! Look at everything multiple times before guessing: the weather, what people like, and which fruits spoil faster.
