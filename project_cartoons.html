Pixel & Data Cartoon Series

A short-form cartoon designed for K-5 learners where two curious robots—Pixel and Data—explore the world and learn data science concepts like collecting data, identifying patterns, visualizing with charts, and thinking critically about fairness and privacy. Episodes end with quick classroom prompts and take-home mini-activities.

Project Goals

Learning outcomes: build early data literacy—ask questions, collect/organize data, read simple charts, spot patterns, and reason about fairness.

Design pillars: 3–5 minute episodes, recurring characters, visual metaphors, interactive “pause & discuss” cues, printable extensions for class and home.

Research lens: measure engagement, comprehension, and self-efficacy; iterate using teacher feedback and classroom pilots.

Accessibility: captions, color-safe palettes, audio descriptions, and adaptable teacher materials.
