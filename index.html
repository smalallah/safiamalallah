About Me

Dr. Safia Malallah is a Teaching Assistant Professor at Kansas State University, specializing in computer science education with a focus on artificial intelligence (AI) and data science from early childhood (PreK-12) through undergraduate levels. As Kansas Ambassador for Women in Data Science and co-director of the D3 Lab, She promotes diversity and inclusion while spearheading statewide efforts to broaden access to data science and AI education throughout Kansas.
