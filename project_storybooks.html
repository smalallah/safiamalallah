Roblox Virtual World Computational Thinking Island

An immersive Roblox world that helps K-12 learners practice core computational thinking (CT) concepts—sequencing, decomposition, abstraction, and debugging—through narrative quests and playful challenges. Built with Roblox Studio and Lua, designed to be inclusive, accessible, and teacher-friendly.

Project Goals

Learning outcomes: help students practice CT concepts in-context, reflect on strategy, and transfer ideas to unplugged/plugged activities.

Design pillars: child-centered narrative, scaffolded quests, immediate feedback, and teacher supports (rubrics, quick-start guides, and printable extensions).

Research lens: we study engagement, self-efficacy, and learning gains, and we iterate on the world using classroom observations and analytics.

Accessibility: color-safe UI, captioned narrative, and input flexibility for mixed devices.
