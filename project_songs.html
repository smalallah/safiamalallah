Computer Science Songs

Research shows that students retain up to twice as much information when learning with music compared to traditional instruction, making songs a powerful tool for teaching complex ideas. This project create an classroom-ready songs that make AI, data science, and core CS concepts catchy and memorable for K-12 learners. Each track includes lyrics, a karaoke version, and a teacher mini-guide with discussion prompts and quick activities.

Learning Goals

Concept mastery: reinforce vocabulary (data, model, pattern), processes (collect → clean → visualize), and big ideas (algorithms, bias, privacy) through repetition and rhyme.

Transfer to activities: each song pairs with a 10-minute unplugged task and a quick reflection prompt.

Accessibility: kid-friendly tempos & keys, captions, lyric sheets, karaoke cuts, and movement cues for multisensory learning.

Assessment: exit tickets and sing-back checks included in the teacher mini-guides.
